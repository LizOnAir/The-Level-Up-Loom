## Background story:

Humans and Pokimons lived together in peace in a world called Fallout. Everyone was happy. Trainers and their Pokimons were best friends.

However, the harmony didn’t last. One day, a strange meteorite crashed into the world, releasing glowing, purple spore cloud with radiation that spread across the land like a plague. 

The radiation changed many Pokimons. They became mutated — angry, feral, and glowing with toxic energy. Especially one creature, Soup. It controlled mutated Pokimons to dominate the world. They attacked everything, wild Pokimons, trained Pokimons, even their human partners.

Humans had to fight to survive. They tamed the remaining healthy Pokimon and trained them for battle. Now, every Trainer is a soldier. Every Pokimon is a weapon and protector.

## Mission
Young Trainers must leave their safe bunkers, explore the radioactive ruins, train pokimons and fight mutated Pokimon to reclaim the world. Their goal: defeat Soup to restore harmony. 

That’s why we invented **The-Level-Up-Loom** that allow trainers to train their Pokimons more efficiently with battlegrounds.
